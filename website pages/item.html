Editor's Notes

"Sunrise, sunset. We all build our days around the sun. A lot is written in a year. Some things might change, but life remains bright."

Diary with no dates; just beautiful minimalist, weekly planner pages using high quality paper. Soft eco PU cover with luxurious gold hot stamping and flexible spine so that it opens flat. Comes with a handy pocket to store notes, ribbon bookmark to mark pages and an elastic band to keep it perfectly closed.
